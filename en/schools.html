Schools · Audio Storytelling

In early years education, children learn through listening, imagination and repetition. Stories, voices and rhythm help shape language, attention and emotional understanding.

This page introduces a simple idea: using screen-free audio storytelling to support classroom routines in Early Years and early primary education — creating calm, focused moments for listening, storytime and independent learning.

What we are exploring

  • Listening & comprehension: how regular audio storytelling supports attention and narrative understanding.

  • Calm routines & autonomy: storytime, listening corners and transitions without screens.
